Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

We want you to love using our premium micro-tools. Since our suite operates via a single annual access model, we offer a transparent satisfaction guarantee.

1. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with the micro-tool selection, access performance, or output layouts within the first 14 days of your initial purchase, we will issue a full refund of your subscription fee.

  • The refund request must be submitted within 14 calendar days from the moment of purchase.
  • No detailed justification is required; simple feedback is highly appreciated to improve our catalog.
  • Refunds do not apply to subsequent annual auto-renewals unless requested within 7 days of the renewal transaction.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please send an email message to our dedicated support channel at support@apextools.io with the following details:

  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  • Your full name used during the secure checkout flow
  • The transaction ID or subscription reference key provided in your email invoice receipt

3. Processing & Transaction Timelines

Once we receive your request, our team will review the transaction date parameters. Approved refunds are processed immediately. The funds will be credited back to your original credit card or payment method within 3 to 5 business days, depending on your financial institution's processing cycles.

4. Abuse of Refund Safeguards

We offer this guarantee in good faith. If we detect patterns of systematic account creation, mass export of files, and subsequent refund requests within a short timeframe, we reserve the right to reject refund requests and blacklist associated access credentials.
