Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

At ApexTools, accessible from index.html, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected and recorded by ApexTools and how we utilize it.

1. Client-Side Data Handling (Sacred Privacy Rule)

Unlike standard SaaS applications, ApexTools processes all tool variables, uploads, values, and settings locally inside your web browser (client-side). None of the files you import or content you generate inside our workspace is ever transmitted to, or stored on, our external servers. Your data stays on your machine.

2. Information We Collect

If you purchase our All-Access Annual Pass, we collect minimal identifiers necessary to provision access and process payments securely. This includes:

  • Email address (for account activation and receipt delivery)
  • Billing details (securely captured via encrypted third-party processor partners)
  • Basic anonymous analytics cookies (to measure site interactions, load performance, and error logs)

3. Cookies and Log Files

ApexTools follows a standard procedure of using log files and cookies. We use cookies to store information about visitors' preferences, records of purchased keys, and which pages the visitor accessed. This information is used to optimize the user experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser types or other indicators.

4. Security Protocols

We implement strict security measures to keep your billing information safe. All transactions are handled through standard payment gateway provider APIs using SSL encryption. We do not store full credit card details on our local database.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

We want to ensure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every subscriber is entitled to the following:

  • The Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your account record emails and billing records.
  • The Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any billing details you believe are inaccurate.
  • The Right to Erasure: You have the right to request that we delete your payment account credentials from our mailing portal.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@apextools.io.
